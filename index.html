About Me

Alex was born and raised in Penang, Malaysia. Growing up in a diversed country allows him to speak three languages (Mandarin, English and Malay) and also other Chinese dialects (Cantonese and Hokkien). He spent his first 20 years in Penang, Malaysia and currently resides in Austin, Texas. Alex graduated from UT Austin in December 2013 and is actively seeking for full-time employment and ready to apply his knowledge and skills to the real world.

Alex received his Associate Degree in Information Systems Engineering from Tunku Abdul Rahman College, Penang in 2010. The college acknowledged his academic excellence by awarding him a Book Prize Award. One year later, he is lucky enough to be admitted to Computer Science program at UT Austin.

Living far away from home country is the best evidence to prove his independence. He is also a self-motivated and organized person.
