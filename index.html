About Me

Alex was born and raised in Penang, Malaysia. Growing up in a diversed country allows him to be able speak three languages (Mandarin, English and Malay) and also other Chinese dialects (Cantonese and Hokkien). He spent his first 20 years in Penang, Malaysia and currently resides in Austin, Texas while pursuing his Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin. Living far away from home country is the best evidence to prove his independence. He is a self-motivated and organized person.
